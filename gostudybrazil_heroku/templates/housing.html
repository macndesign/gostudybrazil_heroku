Housing

Housing and Food

Students will be placed in home-stays, or will have the option to rent apartments. Students will be entitled to three meals a day if they choose the home-stay option, but students who choose the apartment option will be responsible for their own food.

During overnight or extended travel students will stay in Pousada or Hostels. All meals will be included during excursions.
